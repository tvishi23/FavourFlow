Masala Dosa

Vegetarian Breakfast

Servings:

Prep: 12 hr | Cook: 30 min | Total: 12 hr 30 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (200g / 1 cup)
  • 1/3 cup urad dal (70g / 1/3 cup)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 2 boiled potatoes (~300g / 2 medium), mashed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1–2 green chilies, chopped
  • 8–10 curry leaves

Steps:

  1. Soak rice & dal for 6–8 hrs.
  2. Grind into smooth batter, add salt, ferment overnight.
  3. Prepare potato filling with turmeric, mustard seeds, green chilies, curry leaves.
  4. Cook dosa on non-stick pan, add filling, fold, serve hot.

Upma

  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 small onion (~50g), chopped
